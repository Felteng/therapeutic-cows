Why cows are relaxing

Tranquil Animals

The act of touching, petting, hugging, or even just laying next to these calm animals often leads to the body releasing the hormone Oxytocin.

Oxytocin

Oxytocin is known for being the hormone in charge of our happiness and it helps reduce stress. This will often times calm animals down too.

Why cows like us

Showing Affection

Cows love getting spoiled, the more spoiling the more affection. They love getting brushed, playing, chasing balls, sunbathing, and much more!

Affection

When a cow gets affection from a human, they will often show affection back by licking, following you around, or by just being playful.
